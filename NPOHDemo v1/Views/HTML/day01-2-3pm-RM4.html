Established in 1989, the School of Life Sciences and Chemical Technology (LSCT) aims to deliver a robust R&D education and training through intensive lab and field-based training, as well as project and workplace learning. Besides igniting your natural curiosity about the world and fuelling your passion for discovery, LSCT also prepares you for exciting careers in science.

Our five courses are endorsed by industry experts, renowned research institutes and top universities locally and abroad. LSCT also has close links with organisations such as National Parks Board and PUB, Singapore’s National Water Agency. Our strong partnerships will provide you with lots of opportunities to learn in a real-world setting.

Besides having a strong track record in applied research, LSCT is also known for its expertise in areas such as biocatalysis and fermentation, cancer biology, molecular diagnostics, membrane technology, skyrise greening, agritech and aquaculture, and food technology. LSCT’s edge will give you a head start in pursuing research work in these fields.

You will gain a global perspective by participating in various overseas internships and immersion programmes to places such as London, Brisbane, Bangkok, Chongqing, Hong Kong, Nanjing, and Shanghai.
